Belgian Waffles

Sift together 220 grams of flour, 2 teaspoons of baking powder, and 0.5 teaspoon of baking soda. ... Make a well in the flour mixture, and add the milk and eggs. ... Heat a pan over medium heat. ... Once small bubbles form at the center of the pancake, and the edges are brown- flip the pancake.

prep time

30 min.

cook time

10 min.

serving

4 servings

Tags : beef breakfast pancakes food

instructions

step 1

Mix Dry Ingredients: In a Bowl,combine 2 cups all purpose floor, 2 tbs sugar, 1 tbs baking powder, and 1/2 ts salt.

step 2

Prepare wet Ingredients: In a seperate Bowl,whisk together 3/4 cup milk, 1/3 cup vegetable oil, 2 large eggs and 1 ts vanilla extract.

step 3

Gently flod the wet ingredients until just combined.Preheat your waffle iron and Cook the batter according to manufacturer's instructions and Enjoy.

ingredients

2 cups all-purpose flour

1 tablespoon baking powder

2 tablespoon sugar

1/2 teaspoon salt

2 large eggs
