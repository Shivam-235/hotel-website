How do I add a recipe?

  • Fill out the "Add Recipe" form on the main page.
  • Enter the name and description of the recipe.
  • Click "Add Recipe" to submit the form.

Can I edit a recipe?

  • Unfortunately, you cannot edit a recipe once it has been added.
  • To update a recipe, you would need to delete it and then add the updated recipe.

How do I delete a recipe?

  • To delete a recipe, click the "Delete" button on the specific recipe's page.
  • You will be prompted to confirm your decision.
  • Click "OK" to confirm the deletion, or "Cancel" to cancel the deletion.
